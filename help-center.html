Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points? +
You can earn points through various healthcare activities including regular check-ups, preventive care, achieving health goals, and participating in wellness programs.
How do I redeem my points? +
Log into your account, navigate to the rewards section, and choose from available rewards. Points will be automatically deducted from your balance upon redemption.
What rewards are available? +
Rewards include healthcare services, wellness products, fitness memberships, and exclusive health-related experiences. Check our rewards catalog for current offerings.
How long do points last? +
Points are valid for 12 months from the date they are earned. Make sure to use them before they expire!
