Getting Started

  • How do I sign up for Saint Daniels?
    Create your account by selecting your insurance carrier and completing the verification process. You'll need to sign a DocuSign agreement to activate your rewards program membership.
  • What are the eligibility requirements?
    You must be enrolled with a participating insurance carrier and meet their specific eligibility requirements. Keep your account information up to date and maintain active status.

Rewards Program

  • How do I earn rewards?
    Primary rewards are provided through your selected insurance carrier. Additional rewards become available through our program as you participate in healthcare activities.
  • When do I receive my rewards?
    Rewards are credited to your account after completing eligible activities and may vary based on your insurance carrier's specific program terms.

Account Management

  • How do I update my account information?
    Log in to your account dashboard to update your personal information, insurance details, and communication preferences.
  • What if I change insurance carriers?
    Contact our support team to update your insurance information and ensure continued program eligibility.

Our support team is here to assist you with any questions or concerns.
