Understanding Coverage Impact

Higher Coverage Benefits

  • Lower out-of-pocket costs for treatments and procedures
  • More flexibility in choosing specialists and treatment options
  • Access to advanced treatments and preventive care

Limited Coverage Considerations

  • Higher deductibles and out-of-pocket maximums
  • Limited access to specialists and treatment options
  • Longer wait times for non-emergency procedures
